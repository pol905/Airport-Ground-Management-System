About US

Speed, efficiency, and accuracy are important in ground handling services in order to minimize the turnaround time. Faster turnarounds for lower ground times are correlated to better profits. It is imperative that all the information that pertains to the handing of a airplane needs to be accessible to the staff that is servicing the airplane. Not having all the data in a consolidated and easy to view manner increases the turnaround time of a plane. Our aim is to provide a system to ensure faster turnarounds and increase efficiency throughout the airport.
